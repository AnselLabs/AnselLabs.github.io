history

Electrical engineer and artist who loves experimenting with new projects, even when they get challenging. I enjoy blending technology and creativity—whether that’s building tiny miniatures, painting them, designing sound-reactive LED setups, or coding quirky little websites just for fun. I’m fascinated by retro aesthetics, old-school web vibes, and playful ways to mix art and engineering.

Over the years, I’ve collected a variety of nerdy hobbies, from PS2 games like Silent Hill and Resident Evil 4 to scratchbuilding sci-fi and fantasy miniatures. Even when projects feel overwhelming, I love the process of learning, experimenting, and creating little chaotic, fun experiments that combine my curiosity, fandoms, and quirky ideas.
