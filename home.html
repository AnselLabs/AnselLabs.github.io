About Me

Hey, I’m Ansel (he/him) — lifelong gamer, oil painter, and tinkerer of all sorts. Recently, I’ve been diving headfirst into sculpting and miniature painting for RPGs — dice, dragons, and tiny worlds fuel my curiosity. When I’m not coding circuits or painting canvases, you might find me exploring new game mechanics, writing poetry, or experimenting with Linux setups.

This is my little creative lab — a mix of art, code, and whimsy. Stick around and see what sparks your imagination.
